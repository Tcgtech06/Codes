1. GETTING STARTED
================================================================================

Installation
------------

1. Download the Application
   - Clone or download the repository
   - Extract to a folder on your computer

2. Install Python (if not already installed)
   - Download Python 3.8 or higher from python.org
   - During installation, check "Add Python to PATH"

3. Install Dependencies
   - Open Command Prompt or Terminal
   - Navigate to the application folder
   - Run: pip install -r requirements.txt

4. Launch the Application
   - Double-click test.py or run "python test.py"
   - The splash screen will appear for 4 seconds
   - Main application window will open

First Launch
------------

When you first launch the application:
- The splash screen displays the TCG logo and "A PRODUCT OF" text
- After 4 seconds, the main window opens
- You'll see 4 business slots in the sidebar (Business 1-4)
- Each business has a unique color theme

2. UNDERSTANDING THE INTERFACE
================================================================================

Main Window Layout
------------------

The application window is divided into three main sections:

1. Sidebar (Left)
   - Business Buttons: 4 colored buttons for different businesses
     * Business 1: Blue
     * Business 2: Green
     * Business 3: Pink
     * Business 4: Purple
   - Search Bar: Search invoices by ID or client name
   - Invoice History: List of recent invoices (scrollable)

2. Business Information Section (Top Right)
   - Business Name: Enter your business name
   - Address: Multi-line address field
   - Email: Business email address
   - Phone: Business contact number
   - GST Number: Optional GST registration number (right side)
   - Watermark: Optional watermark text for invoices (right side)
   - Logo Upload: Button to upload business logo
   - Save Info: Button to save business details

3. Invoice Creation Section (Bottom Right)
   - Client Details: Name and phone number
   - Invoice Details: Invoice number, date, status
   - Items Table: Add products/services with quantity and price
   - Calculations: Discount, tax, totals
   - Action Buttons: Generate PDF, Generate Word, Preview

3. SETTING UP YOUR BUSINESS
================================================================================

Step 1: Select a Business Slot
-------------------------------

1. Click on one of the 4 business buttons in the sidebar
2. The button will highlight to show it's selected
3. The business information section will load existing data (if any)

Step 2: Enter Business Information
-----------------------------------

1. Business Name
   - Enter your official business name
   - This appears at the top of invoices

2. Address
   - Enter your complete business address
   - Use multiple lines if needed
   - Include city, state, and PIN code

3. Email
   - Enter your business email
   - This appears on invoices for client contact

4. Phone
   - Enter your business phone number
   - Include country code if needed

5. GST Number (Optional)
   - Located on the right side
   - Enter your GST registration number
   - Appears on invoices if provided
   - Leave blank if not applicable

6. Watermark (Optional)
   - Located on the right side below GST
   - Enter custom text to appear at bottom of invoices
   - Examples: "Thank you for your business", "Terms apply"
   - Use ASCII characters only (avoid special symbols)

Step 3: Upload Business Logo
-----------------------------

1. Click the "Upload Logo" button
2. Select an image file (JPG or PNG)
3. Recommended size: 500x500 pixels or smaller
4. The logo will appear on all invoices for this business

Step 4: Save Business Information
----------------------------------

1. Click the "Save Info" button
2. A success message will appear
3. Your business details are now saved
4. You can switch to other businesses and return anytime

Managing Multiple Businesses
-----------------------------

You can manage up to 4 different businesses:
- Each business has independent settings
- Each business has its own invoice numbering
- Each business has its own invoice history
- Each business has its own logo and branding
- Switch between businesses using the sidebar buttons

================================================================================
4. CREATING YOUR FIRST INVOICE
================================================================================

Step 1: Enter Client Details
-----------------------------

1. Client Name
   - Enter the customer's name or company name
   - Required field

2. Client Phone
   - Enter the customer's phone number
   - Saved in history for future reference
   - Optional but recommended

Step 2: Set Invoice Details
----------------------------

1. Invoice Number
   - Auto-generated based on last invoice
   - You can modify if needed
   - Must be unique

2. Date
   - Defaults to today's date
   - Click to change if needed
   - Format: DD/MM/YYYY

3. Status
   - Choose "Paid" or "Pending"
   - Pending invoices show in RED and BOLD
   - Helps track unpaid invoices

Step 3: Add Invoice Items
--------------------------

1. Item Description
   - Enter product or service name
   - Be specific (e.g., "Chocolate Cake 1kg")

2. Enable Quantity (Optional)
   - Check the "Quantity" checkbox to enable quantity-based pricing
   - If unchecked, enter total amount directly

3. Quantity (if enabled)
   - Select unit from dropdown:
     * gm: Grams
     * Kg: Kilograms
     * Litre: Liters
     * Piece: Individual items
     * Rounds: Delivery rounds
     * Trips: Transport trips
     * Custom: Enter your own unit
   
   - Enter quantity value:
     * Whole numbers: 1, 2, 10
     * Decimals: 15.5, 0.5, 2.75
     * Fractions: 1/2, 1/4, 3/4, 1/3
   
   - Examples:
     * "1/2 Kg" = Half kilogram
     * "0.5 Kg" = Half kilogram
     * "15.5 gm" = 15.5 grams
     * "10 Piece" = 10 pieces

4. Amount/Price
   - If quantity is disabled: Enter total amount
   - If quantity is enabled: Enter price per unit
   - System auto-calculates: Quantity x Price = Total

5. Add Item
   - Click "Add Item" button
   - Item appears in the table below
   - Repeat for all items

Step 4: Apply Discount and Tax
-------------------------------

1. Discount
   - Enter discount percentage (0-100)
   - Applied to subtotal
   - Leave as 0 for no discount

2. Tax
   - Enter tax percentage (e.g., 18 for 18% GST)
   - Applied after discount
   - Leave as 0 for no tax

3. Auto-Calculation
   - Subtotal = Sum of all items
   - Discount Amount = Subtotal x (Discount% / 100)
   - Taxable Amount = Subtotal - Discount
   - Tax Amount = Taxable Amount x (Tax% / 100)
   - Grand Total = Taxable Amount + Tax Amount

Step 5: Preview Invoice
------------------------

1. Click the "Preview" button
2. A dialog window opens showing the invoice
3. Review all details carefully
4. Check calculations
5. Close preview when satisfied

Step 6: Generate Invoice
-------------------------

Choose one of the following options:

Generate PDF
1. Click "Generate PDF" button
2. Choose save location
3. Enter filename
4. PDF is created with:
   - Business logo and details
   - Client information
   - Itemized list with quantities
   - Discount and tax breakdown
   - Grand total with Rs symbol
   - Watermark at bottom (if set)
   - GST number (if provided)

Generate Word Document
1. Click "Generate Word" button
2. Choose save location
3. Enter filename
4. Word document (.docx) is created with same details as PDF

Step 7: Invoice Saved to History
---------------------------------

- Invoice is automatically saved to history
- Appears in the sidebar under the business
- Can be searched and loaded later

5. ADVANCED FEATURES
================================================================================

Quantity System
---------------

The quantity system allows flexible pricing:

Without Quantity
- Simple total amount entry
- Good for fixed-price services
- Example: "Website Design - Rs50,000"

With Quantity
- Price per unit x quantity
- Supports multiple units
- Auto-calculates total

Supported Units:
- gm (grams): For small weights
- Kg (kilograms): For larger weights
- Litre: For liquids
- Piece: For countable items
- Rounds: For delivery services
- Trips: For transport services
- Custom: Define your own unit

Input Formats:
- Whole: 1, 2, 10, 100
- Decimal: 0.5, 15.5, 2.75
- Fraction: 1/2, 1/4, 3/4, 1/3, 2/3

Examples:
Item: Chocolate Cake
Quantity: 1/2 Kg
Price: Rs800 per Kg
Total: Rs400

Item: Cupcakes
Quantity: 24 Piece
Price: Rs50 per Piece
Total: Rs1,200

Item: Milk
Quantity: 2.5 Litre
Price: Rs60 per Litre
Total: Rs150

Watermark Feature
-----------------

Add custom text at the bottom of invoices:

1. Enter watermark text in Business Information section (right side)
2. Text appears at bottom of PDF and Word documents
3. Displayed in gray, italic, centered
4. Use for:
   - Thank you messages
   - Terms and conditions
   - Payment instructions
   - Return policy notes

Tips:
- Keep it short (1-2 lines)
- Use ASCII characters only
- Avoid special symbols that may cause PDF errors
- Examples:
  * "Thank you for your business!"
  * "Payment due within 30 days"
  * "All sales are final"

GST Number Display
------------------

If you enter a GST number:
- Appears on the right side above the date in invoices
- Format: "GST: [Your Number]"
- Helps with tax compliance
- Leave blank if not applicable

Pending Status
--------------

Mark invoices as pending to track unpaid bills:

1. Select "Pending" from status dropdown
2. Invoice shows in RED and BOLD in:
   - Preview dialog
   - PDF documents
   - Word documents
   - Invoice history
3. Easy to identify unpaid invoices at a glance

6. MANAGING INVOICE HISTORY
================================================================================

Viewing History
---------------

- All invoices appear in the sidebar under the business
- Shows: Invoice ID, Client Name, Date, Amount
- Scrollable list (unlimited invoices)
- Color-coded by business theme

Searching Invoices
------------------

Use the search bar to find specific invoices:

1. Search by Invoice ID
   - Type the invoice number (e.g., "1005")
   - Exact or partial match

2. Search by Client Name
   - Type client name (e.g., "Mohan")
   - Case-insensitive
   - Partial match supported

3. Real-Time Filtering
   - Results update as you type
   - Clear search to see all invoices

4. No Results
   - Shows "No invoices found" if no matches
   - Shows "No recent invoices" if history is empty

Loading Previous Invoices
--------------------------

1. Click on an invoice in the history list
2. All details load into the form:
   - Client name and phone
   - Invoice number and date
   - All items with quantities
   - Discount and tax
   - Status
3. Modify as needed
4. Generate new invoice or update existing

Deleting Invoices
-----------------

Currently, invoices cannot be deleted from the UI. To remove invoices:

1. Close the application
2. Open DB/history.json in a text editor
3. Carefully remove the invoice entry
4. Save the file
5. Restart the application

Warning: Be careful when editing JSON files manually. Invalid JSON will cause errors.

8. TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Application Won't Start
-----------------------

Problem: Double-clicking test.py does nothing

Solutions:
1. Check Python is installed: python --version
2. Install dependencies: pip install -r requirements.txt
3. Run from command line: python test.py
4. Check for error messages in console

PDF Generation Fails
--------------------

Problem: Error when clicking "Generate PDF"

Solutions:
1. Check DejaVuSans.ttf fonts are in the root folder
2. Use ASCII characters only in watermark
3. Verify all required fields are filled
4. Check invoice number is valid
5. Try without watermark first

Common Errors:
- "Font not found": Download DejaVu fonts
- "Unicode error": Remove special characters from watermark
- "File in use": Close any open PDF files

Logo Not Displaying
-------------------

Problem: Logo doesn't appear on invoices

Solutions:
1. Check file format (JPG or PNG only)
2. Verify file size (under 5MB recommended)
3. Re-upload the logo
4. Check DB/logos/ folder for the file
5. Try a different image

Calculations Are Wrong
----------------------

Problem: Totals don't match expectations

Solutions:
1. Verify quantity format (use 0.5 instead of .5)
2. Check price per unit vs total amount
3. Ensure quantity checkbox is enabled/disabled correctly
4. Verify discount and tax percentages
5. Use preview to see breakdown

Search Not Working
------------------

Problem: Search bar doesn't find invoices

Solutions:
1. Check spelling of client name
2. Try partial search (first few letters)
3. Search is case-insensitive
4. Clear search and try again
5. Verify invoices exist in history

Invoice History Not Loading
----------------------------

Problem: Previous invoices don't appear

Solutions:
1. Check DB/history.json exists
2. Verify JSON file is not corrupted
3. Restore from backup if needed
4. Check correct business is selected
5. Restart the application

Watermark on Wrong Page
------------------------

Problem: Watermark appears on page 2

Solutions:
1. Reduce number of invoice items
2. Shorten item descriptions
3. Use shorter watermark text
4. This is a layout issue with many items

Business Theme Wrong Color
---------------------------

Problem: Business 3 shows orange instead of pink

Solutions:
1. Check DB/profiles.json file
2. Verify color code is #ec4899 for biz_3
3. Restart the application
4. Re-save business information

Data Loss Prevention
--------------------

Problem: Lost all invoices or business data

Prevention:
1. Regular backups of DB folder
2. Don't manually edit JSON files unless necessary
3. Close application properly (don't force quit)
4. Keep backup before major changes

Recovery:
1. Restore from most recent backup
2. Check if DB/history.json.bak exists
3. Contact support if critical data is lost

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Can I use this for multiple businesses?
Yes! You can manage up to 4 different businesses, each with its own branding, invoices, and settings.

Is my data secure?
All data is stored locally on your computer. No data is sent to external servers. You are responsible for backing up your data.

Can I customize the invoice layout?
The current version has a fixed modern layout. Customization requires modifying the Python code.

How many invoices can I store?
Unlimited! Each business can store as many invoices as your hard drive allows.

Can I export data to Excel?
Not directly. You can manually copy data from the JSON files or generate Word documents and convert them.

Does it work offline?
Yes! The application works completely offline. No internet connection required.

Can I print invoices?
Yes! Generate a PDF and print it using your PDF viewer's print function.
